Lasagna Recipe



Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that layers pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and gooey mozzarella, all baked to perfection

This hearty meal is perfect for family gatherings, potlucks, or a cozy dinner at home.

Ingredients

Meat Sauce

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 500g ground beef
  • 400g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Béchamel Sauce

  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups milk
  • A pinch of nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Additional

  • 12 lasagna sheets
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce:
    • Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
    • Add onion and garlic; sauté until softened.
    • Add ground beef; cook until browned.
    • Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
    • Simmer for 20 minutes.
  2. Prepare the Béchamel Sauce:
    • Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
    • Whisk in flour; cook for 1-2 minutes.
    • Gradually whisk in milk; cook until thickened.
    • Season with nutmeg, salt, and pepper.
  3. Cook the Lasagna Sheets:
    • Cook lasagna sheets according to package instructions.
    • Drain and set aside.
  4. Assemble the Lasagna:
    • Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
    • Layer with lasagna sheets, meat sauce, béchamel sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan.
    • Repeat layers, ending with a generous layer of cheese on top.
  5. Bake:
    • Cover with aluminum foil; bake for 25 minutes.
    • Remove foil; bake for an additional 20-25 minutes until golden and bubbly.
  6. Serve:
    • Let lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
